Donate Your Smile

Donate your smile?

Simply smile and you donated. Weather you are waiting for an elevator or queuing you can now create better outcomes for yourself and others. It works by overlaying a sponsored filter on your face triggered by your smile. The ad revenue is what pays for the donations. And allowing users to extended their experience via our qr code where they can retrieve voucher or shop directly for example.
